## Overview

A cohesive visual identity system for the Fast X soundtrack rollout, designed to balance the franchise's cinematic intensity with platform-specific adaptability across Spotify, Apple Music, and YouTube.

## The Challenge

The Fast X soundtrack needed a visual language that felt fresh while honoring the franchise's massive legacy. The assets effectively needed to:
*   **Adapt:** Function seamlessly across 16:9 cinematic promos, 1:1 cover art, and 9:16 vertical social clips.
*   **Unify:** Tie together a diverse lineup of artists under one visual umbrella.
*   **Engage:** Drive streams through motion-first assets on social media.

## The Solution

I collaborated with the SVP of Marketing at APG to develop a modular design system. Instead of treating each asset as a one-off, we built a flexible framework:

### 1. Single Release System
I created a unified grid layout for single covers. This system allowed us to swap artist imagery and typography while maintaining a consistent "Fast X" look across both static artwork and motion visuals for social platforms.
