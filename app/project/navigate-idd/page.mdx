# Navigate IDD

**Role:** AI Systems Product Designer | **Timeline:** Sep 2025 - Oct 2025  
**Tech Stack:** n8n, OpenAI GPT-4 & Claude Sonnet 4.5, Pinecone, Synthesia

---

## Overview

Navigate IDD is a governed AI system designed to transform complex Medicaid waiver regulations into verified, multilingual educational videos. In a domain where accuracy is critical, this system bridges the gap between dense policy documents and accessible family guidance, ensuring compliance while drastically reducing content production time.

## The Challenge

Families seeking disability services often face a barrier of dense regulations and confusing waiver language.
*   **Accessibility:** Critical information is locked in complex legal text, often unavailable in languages other than English.
*   **Scalability:** Traditional video production is slow, expensive, and difficult to update when policies change.
*   **Compliance:** Generative AI solutions often struggle with "hallucination," which is unacceptable in regulated healthcare environments.

## The Solution

I architected and built an end-to-end **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** pipeline that grounds AI outputs strictly in verified policy documents. The system automates the creation of scripts, translations, and video production, all while maintaining a rigid human-in-the-loop governance model.
